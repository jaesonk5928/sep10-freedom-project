Current Business Soft/Hardware Tools

Asana: This business software tool provides features like tasks to do, deadline submissions, progress tracker, and communication all in one location. Asana was founded in 2008 by Dustin Moskovitz and Justin Rosenstein.
Zoom: This software tool helps businesses interact and talk with each other by audio/video call conferences, even if you are far from the person. People can also share their screens, files, and text each other. Zoom was founded in 2012 by Eric Yuan.
Slack: This software helped people communicate with friends and family, similar to Discord. It can help engage in conversations, submit projects. Slack was founded in 2013 by Stewart Butterfield.
